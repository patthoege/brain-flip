Brain Flip

Memory Card Game

Choose a difficulty level

Challenge yourself by choosing two cards at a time and playing against your memory.

How to Play ?

  1. Start by choosing a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  2. Once you've selected your difficulty, the cards will be displayed face down.
  3. Click on any card to reveal its image.
  4. Click on another card to reveal its image as well.
  5. If the two cards have the same image, they will remain face up, and you've made a match!
  6. If the two cards don't match, they will flip back face down, and you can try again.
  7. Keep flipping pairs of cards until you've matched all of them.
  8. Your goal is to match all the cards in the shortest time possible and with the fewest moves.
